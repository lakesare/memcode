Hello, dear Memcode users!
In the past few months Memcode has seen a number of important updates.
As always - if you have some questions, simply respond to this email, we read everything.

New stuff

• Bright Theme Improvements
The bright theme looks really good now.
• Text-to-Speech Support
Memcode can read flashcards aloud now!
Our voicing (backed by OpenAI's TTS) supports all widespread languages - English, German, Japanese, et cetera.
To enable text-to-speech, click on the audio button on the flashcard review page.
• LLM Integration
In the Import/Export modal (flashcard creation page ➤ IMPORT), you can:
1) copy the correct-flashcard-format prompt for your LLM
2) feed this prompt to your LLM of choice
3) import the plaintext flashcards LLM created for you
You can now use Memcode with your favorite LLM!
• New way to review flashcards: Just-click
In the Settings modal (hover over your avatar ➤ Settings), you now have a setting for letting you CLICK on the hidden word to reveal it.
This is useful for when you're on your phone and do not feel like typing out the full answer.
• Email Signups
You can now sign up by email (or just username) & password on Memcode.
This is important for people who value privacy, and simplifies memcode setup for schools.

Bug fixes

• "Image stuck loading" fix
You can click wherever when your image is uploading; you can upload multiple images in different flashcards at the same time, etc. - this will all work.
• Image compression
From now on, the images you upload will be automatically compressed (converted to max 600px in width&height). This doesn't affect the quality of the displayed images (all of our interfaces are under 600px), but reduces the loading time of courses.
You don't have to think about the image size before uploading it anymore!
• Flashcard order fixed
Flashcard ordering & reordering was working weirdly - this is fully fixed now.
SHIFT-clicking to select multiple flashcards works perfectly now too.

New Courses

We just crafted a beginner language course for 20 languages (German, Italian, Spanish, etc.) - check them out at memcode.com/courses!
